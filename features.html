Xali's features

I) Usage resume

This document resume all features of Xali. Before that, a resume of how this platform will be used.


  • Xali will be hosted on a Web server, maybe by an organisation, maybe not.

  • On this platform, there is a root who can manage all the platform.

  • Each organisation will have an account on, gived by the root.

  • Each organisation will can manage its volunteers, its camps, its employees.

  • The volunteers or employees will add survivors in database, research them too.

  • Once a person will be with her family, the volunteers or employees will can turn the person as «with her family »

  • Organisations will can add camps where they are installed, where there are their volunteers, their survivors.

  • Organisation will can tell where there is a war nearly of them.

II) Features resume

    Here you'll see the features of Xali.

  • Root can create organisations

  • Organisation can add camps, volunteers and employees

  • Organisation can affect volunteers and employees at one camp

  • Volunteers and employees can add survivors in one camp

  • Volunteers and employees can research survivors in database

  • They can also update the survivors's profile to « with their family »
